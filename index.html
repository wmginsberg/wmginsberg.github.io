About

I'm Wendy, a left-handed, lizard-loving Product Manager living in San Francisco, CA.

I am currently working on the awesome Polymer Project as part of the Chrome Web Platform at Google. Our goal is to help web developers make the most of the web platform by leveraging the most innovative web APIs in modern browsers. I am also a big fan of open source development and try to work on silly side projects whenever I can.
