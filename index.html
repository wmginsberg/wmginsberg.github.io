Wendy Ginsberg

About

I'm Wendy. I'm currently based in the US working at LinkedIn as the Director of Product for Premium, Value. Previously, I worked at Slice as the Senior Director of Product for our Consumer Products. I spent 2 years as the Principal Product Manager for Personal Banking at Monzo, and 5 years as a PM at Google, half in London and half in San Francisco, where I worked across Hardware, Chrome, and Maps. I was born and raised in New York, but have lived and worked across the U.S. in 7 states. I also spent 5 years living in London.
